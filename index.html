Code Blooded

Hello! We are team Code Blooded #12649. We are an FTC team based in Southwest Minneapolis, Minnesota. Currently we have ten members half of whom are seniors and the other half sophmores.

Our team wants to make a positive impact on the FIRST community by encouraging and mentoring students of all backgrounds to participate and thrive in robotics.
